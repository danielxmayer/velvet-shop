Most souvenir shops
on Prague's Royal Way
are not Czech.

We believe Czech culture deserves better. We curate boxes of authentic Czech culture — handmade puppets, retro foods, samizdat stories and artisan prints, all made in the Czech Republic and chosen for their story, not their tourist appeal.

Each object comes with context: who made it, where it comes from, and why it matters today — so you don't just take home an object, you take home a piece of lived Czech experience.

Why we exist

We don't sell souvenirs —
we deliver truth
from real Czech lives.

Václav Havel spent his life insisting on authenticity over performance. He wrote plays by hand, passed manuscripts through underground networks, and spent years in prison for believing that words — real words, true words — matter.

That same principle guides every item we choose. We don't sell tourism. We don't curate what's convenient. We find the things that have a story worth telling, the makers who still work by hand, the foods whose recipes haven't changed in a century.

Each box is a piece of lived Czech experience. Open it slowly.
